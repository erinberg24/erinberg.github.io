2012 NFL Season: Sentiment Analysis of Game Outcomes and Predicting Wins Using Fans’ Tweets

Erin Berg, Keith Carroll, Courtney Peterson, and John Whalen
Course Project Technique

The goals of this project were to find connection between fan sentiment and the football game outcome as well as predict who ended up winning based on tweets after a game. We used post-game tweets from the 2012 NFL season, analyzed, and classified them to obtain our results.
